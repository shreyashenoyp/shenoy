Dish Dash
Year ✦ 2023
UX Research and UI Design
Description

GJB cafeteria's overcrowding during peak hours leads to missed orders and seating issues. A solution was needed to improve the dining experience.

Introducing Dishdash, your campus dining solution. Streamline your food ordering, check real-time crowd levels, and reserve tables with ease. Faculty members can enjoy convenient food delivery right to their office. Plus, our eco-friendly online billing helps reduce paper waste.
