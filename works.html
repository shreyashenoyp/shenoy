Izmenyat: The board game
Year ✦ 2023
Educational Board Game Design
Description

An immersive tabletop strategy game set during the chaotic October Revolution of 1917.

Designed for ages 13-16, players navigate the tumultuous events leading to the Bolshevik's rise to power. With secret alliances, resource management, and intense bidding phases, players must solve historical challenges and secure key documents to gain control of Russia. Will you lead the Bolsheviks to victory, or will the White Army reclaim the Manifesto?
